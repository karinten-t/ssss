ABOUT US

At OSUPUKO TOURS & PHOTOGRAPHY, we believe that every journey is a story waiting to be told, and every moment is a memory worth capturing. Founded by a team of passionate adventurers and professional photographers, we are dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences that blend the thrill of exploration with the artistry of photography.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to guide you through the world’s most breathtaking landscapes and to capture the essence of your journey in stunning, timeless photographs. Whether you’re trekking through the wild savannahs, hiking up majestic mountains, or simply soaking in the beauty of nature, we are here to make your adventure extraordinary.

What We Offer

  • Guided Tours: Our experienced safari guides will take you off the beaten path to discover hidden gems and iconic landmarks. From wildlife safaris to cultural excursions, we curate tours that cater to your sense of adventure.
  • Professional Photography: Our team of skilled photographers will document your journey, ensuring that every smile, every sunset, and every thrilling moment is preserved in high-quality images.
  • Personalized Experiences: We understand that no two travelers are the same. That’s why we tailor our tours and photography sessions to match your unique preferences and style.

Why Choose Us?

  • Passion for Adventure: We live and breathe exploration. Our love for the wild drives us to create experiences that are as authentic as they are exhilarating.
  • Expertise: With years of experience in both tourism and photography, we know how to make your journey seamless and memorable.
  • Commitment to Excellence: From the moment you book with us until the final photo is delivered, we are committed to providing exceptional service and unforgettable memories.

Our Team

Led by FRANCIS LITASON KAMOIRO (@FLK), a seasoned safari guide and photographer, our team is a blend of adventure enthusiasts and creative storytellers. Together, we bring a wealth of knowledge, creativity, and passion to every tour and photography session.

Join Us

Whether you’re a solo traveler, a couple, or a group of friends, OSUPUKO TOURS & PHOTOGRAPHY invites you to embark on a journey like no other. Let us guide you through the wild, capture your most precious moments, and help you create memories that will last a lifetime.
